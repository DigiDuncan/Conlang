## Vowels
- a, i, u, e, o, schwa
- It's like the Japanese ones + schwa (uh)
- It's fine, it works

## Consonants
- m, h, p, k
- n (can only be used at the end of a word[^1])
## Syllables
Syllables are a vowel, optionally preceded by a consonant, optionally succeeded by a "ending consonant" (either "n" or "p".)

[^1]: subject to change
